# Privacy Policy for Split Soup

**Last updated: February 16, 2026**

## Overview

Split Soup ("the App") is a minimalist event sharing application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Username (chosen by you)

### User-Generated Content
- Events you create (title, date, description)
- Friend connections you establish
- Alert messages you send to friends

### Automatically Collected
- Device type (for notifications)
- Push notification tokens (if notifications are enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Send event reminders and notifications
- Enable event sharing between friends
- Improve the App experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider with encryption at rest and in transit. Servers are located in the United States.

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes

We **may** share data:
- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your data through the App
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Opt out of notifications at any time

To delete your account, contact us at the email below.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses:
- **Supabase** - Database and authentication ([supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Expo** - App infrastructure ([expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy))

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.

---

*Split Soup is committed to protecting your privacy.*
