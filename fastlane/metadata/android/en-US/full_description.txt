Split Soup is a minimalist app for sharing events with friends.

Features:
- Create and manage events with your friends
- Send event invitations by username
- Accept or decline invites from friends
- Get reminders for upcoming events
- Send quick alerts to your friends
- Real-time updates when events change

Privacy-focused:
- No ads or tracking
- Your data stays on secure servers
- Minimal permissions required

Perfect for coordinating meetups, group activities, and staying in sync with the people who matter.
