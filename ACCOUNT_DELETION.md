# Account Deletion Request

## Split Soup

To request deletion of your Split Soup account and all associated data, please email:

**orlandocloss1@gmail.com**

Include the following in your email:
- Subject line: "Split Soup Account Deletion Request"
- The email address associated with your account

Your request will be processed within 7 days. Upon deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:
- Your profile and username
- Events you created
- Event invitations
- Friend connections
- Alert messages

---

*Split Soup respects your right to data deletion.*
